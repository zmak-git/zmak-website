Roadmap

Phase 1
ZMAK Team, Board of Advisors and Roadmap

The assembly of a professional and executive team for ZMAK, in creating and finalising the Roadmap, which will set the start to end strategy of the Zombies x Mutant Ape Kids. Reeling in reputable partners with different fields of expertise and experience, ZMAK will run like a business with various departments running up individual key components (Design, Game Development, Legal, Marketing, & Strategic Resources).

Phase 2
Phase 2
ZMAK NFT Lifepass

The ZMAK NFT Lifepass will provide prestigious and exclusive benefits to all holders. Broken down into 7 different tiers, all lifepasses are created to provide their respective levels of benefits to holders. These have been designed to allow all individuals, regardless of demographics, to be able to gain access to both the real-life and virtual growth of the ZMAK events and gamified ecosystem.

Phase 3
Phase 3
ZMAK – Zombie NFT Series

The official ZMAK - Zombie NFT Series will consist of the most incredibly well designed and innovative pieces of art, and whilst the artwork and concept are clear in being zombie focused, we will aim to ensure we create one of the most diverse and inclusive pieces of art that have been created and showcased to the world. This artwork, launched at different timings throughout the project, will be implemented directly into ZMAK Move-To-Earn game, meaning your own investment can be a playable character throughout the world.

Phase 4
Phase 4
ZMAK RFYL Zombie Run Event

Run For Your Lives Zombie Run events, will make a comeback and be held in various parts of the world to grow ZMAK as a brand. Bringing back the Zombie Run elements where people can partake in fun and casual physical run events, helping truly create our motto and vision of ‘Be Active, Be Social, Be United’. We will continue to look to create worldwide opportunities for the various community members throughout the world to take part in these incredible events.

Phase 5
Phase 5
Tokenomics and Energy Coin ($EC)

The development of ZMAK tokenomics and Energy Coin ($EC) will form a huge part of the ZMAK Move-To-Earn gamified ecosystem. The ideology and focus of creating the Energy Coin ($EC) are simple, give value to those participating and investing both financially and physically to the ZMAK community. By the nature of the gamified ecosystem that will exist, the end-user will benefit from their time invested while ensuring they live an active lifestyle.

Phase 6
Phase 6
ZMAK Move-To-Earn Game Beta Launch

The official beta testing of the ZMAK Move-To-Earn game for users to immerse themselves into, to earn Energy Coins ($EC) by exercising such as running. Early exclusive access will be granted to the ZMAK NFT Lifepass holders while ZMAK Zombie NFT holders are able to utilize their NFT as a playable character in the game.

Phase 7
Phase 7
ZMAK Official Merchandise Store

The ZMAK official merchandise store will become live and open for purchasing, both utilising the in-game currency (Energy Coins - $EC), cryptocurrency and fiat currencies. This will include a vast range of branded items and collectibles such as ZMAK branded apparel, accessories, figurines, and collectibles.

Phase 8
Global ZMAK Themed Clubhouse

ZMAK NFT Lifepass holders will now have exclusive access to ZMAK Clubhouses around the world. Built as a networking hub, its main goal is to enable ZMAK community to liaise with like-minded individuals, seek expert business advice and form business partnerships. It also aims to be one of the first physical clubhouses for the most innovative creators within the NFT environment, serving as a gateway for newcomers, and a home of the experts.
